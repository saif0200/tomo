**No lock-in. No limits. Your choice.**

Tomo is a lightweight desktop overlay powered by AI models running on your terms, run them locally with Ollama and llama.cpp for complete privacy, or bring your own keys to OpenRouter, Claude, and more. Double-tap Control ⌃ to summon Tomo from anywhere. Ask a question, get an answer, and get back to work.
